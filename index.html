Santokki begins with the Korean landscapes we love most: pine forests, damp earth after rain, soft tea notes, and gentle woods. We shape these memories into modern scents for European homes.

Our collection is proudly vegan and cruelty-free from formula to final use. No animal-derived ingredients, no animal testing, and no compromise on quality. Every product is designed to feel clean, calm, and intentional while staying responsible in modern daily life.
